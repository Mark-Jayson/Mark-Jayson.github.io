Welcome to Switzerland

Switzerland, officially the Swiss Confederation, is a landlocked country located in west-central Europe. It is bordered by Italy to the south, France to the west, Germany to the north, and Austria and Liechtenstein to the east. Switzerland is geographically divided among the Swiss Plateau, the Alps and the Jura; the Alps occupy the greater part of the territory, whereas most of the country's population of 9 million are concentrated on the plateau, which hosts its largest cities and economic centres, including Zurich, Geneva, and Base

About Switzerland

Switzerland, a land of breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage, offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler. From the majestic Alps to pristine lakes, charming villages to cosmopolitan cities, Switzerland is a paradise waiting to be explored.

Top Attractions

Here are some of the best places to visit in Switzerland

Matterhorn

Matterhorn

The iconic pyramid-shaped peak in the Swiss Alps, standing at 4,478 meters (14,692 ft). It's one of the highest summits in the Alps and Europe.

Lake Geneva

Lake Geneva

A crescent-shaped lake shared between Switzerland and France. It's surrounded by the Alps and is known for its luxurious resorts and picturesque towns.

Chillon Castle

Chillon Castle

A medieval fortress on the shores of Lake Geneva. It's one of Switzerland's most visited historic monuments, with a history spanning over 1000 years.
